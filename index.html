Waste Reduction

Millions of tons of potentially reusable materials are discarded annually. By buying these non-consumable parts and reselling them to manufacturers, we help reduce the volume of waste that ends up in landfills

Pollution Reduction

Creation of Green Jobs

We contribute to the economy by creating jobs in the recycling and green sectors, which are crucial for sustainable development, reintroducing recycled materials into the manufacturing process

How It Works

How Our Platform Works

  • For Sellers

    As a seller, you can turn your waste materials into profit. Simply register on our platform, select your raw materials or waste product for disposal, and drop them off at a nearby agent location. Once an agent validates your materials, you get paid immediately.

  • For Agents

    Agents play a crucial role in our network by validating and collecting raw materials. Join our platform, set up your profile, and earn commissions based on your commitment and the quantity of materials you validate

  • For Industries

    Make orders for raw materials needed for your industry, Browse available materials, place your order, and choose your preferred shipping option

Services

Comprehensive Recycling Solutions

Buying Raw Materials from People

We purchase a variety of raw materials directly from individuals and smaller companies. By doing so, we ensure that these materials are put to good use rather than being discarded. Our straightforward process allows suppliers to monetize their waste efficiently.

Purchasing Waste Products

Our platform is committed to reducing waste by buying non-consumable parts and waste products from the public. This not only helps in minimizing landfill waste but also supports a circular economy where materials are continually repurposed.

Agent Validation and Payment

Agents are the backbone of our operation, ensuring the quality and authenticity of the raw materials and waste products. We pay our agents for their crucial role in validating and collecting materials, ensuring a seamless and efficient process.

Selling to Industries

We supply high-quality, recycled raw materials and waste products to manufacturing industries. Our service helps industries reduce their reliance on virgin materials, thus saving energy and conserving natural resources.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up as a seller?

Signing up is easy. Just visit our registration page, provide your NIN and account details, select the raw material or waste product you have and the quantity for sell

What are the requirements for agents?

Agents need to register with their NIN, BVN, and account details. They will be responsible for validating and collecting raw materials/Waste from sellers

How does the payment process work for manufacturing Indusries?

Manufacturing Indusries place orders on our platform and make payments to the company. Depending on the chosen option, and quantity materials can be shipped or picked up locally

What types of materials do you accept?

We accept a wide range of non-consumable raw materials that can be recycled and reused by manufacturers. For a detailed list, please visit our accepted materials page.
